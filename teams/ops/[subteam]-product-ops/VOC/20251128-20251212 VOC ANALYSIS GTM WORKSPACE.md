### üîπ BASELINE FEATURE REQUESTS

**These requests have appeared across multiple reporting periods.**

#### Theme \#1: Seamless Navigation Between Workspace and Standard Views

**Status:** ‚Üí Stable **Requested by:** 8 accounts this period

**What customers want:** The ability to easily jump from Workspace account lists back to standard ZoomInfo company pages or Salesforce records where reps conduct actual selling activities, without losing context or having to search manually.

**Specific requests:**

- Hyperlinked company names that click through to full ZoomInfo profiles \- **Cvent**, **eGain**, **G2**  
- Integration with Salesforce that maintains bidirectional sync of notes and activities \- **Frost Bank**, **Brex**, **MaintainX**  
- Ability to open accounts in side panel without leaving Workspace view \- **Illumio**, **Applied Innovation**

**Priority:** **High**

**Rationale:** While request volume decreased slightly (18 accounts in Nov 3-17 vs 8 this period), this remains critical for workflow adoption. The accounts requesting this tend to be more technically sophisticated, suggesting this is a blocker for power users rather than mainstream customers. Addressing this enhances adoption among most valuable customer segment.

#### Theme \#2: Access to Salesforce Custom Objects for Complete Intelligence

**Status:** ‚Üí Stable **Requested by:** 6 accounts this period

**What customers want:** AI-powered insights that incorporate data from Salesforce custom objects where critical business information lives (product usage, support tickets, projects, customer complaints, renewal dates, etc.) beyond standard account/opportunity/contact objects.

**Specific requests:**

- Read access to custom objects containing product usage, complaints, and projects data \- **Frost Bank**, **Brex**  
- Ability to surface insights from custom relationship structures and buying committees \- **MaintainX**, **GAF Corp.**  
- Incorporate renewal and support ticket data into prioritization logic \- **Alation Inc.**, **Illumio**

**Priority:** **High**

**Rationale:** Consistent request volume (6 accounts both periods) indicates ongoing need. For existing customer expansion plays, the AI's recommendations feel incomplete without understanding product adoption, support interactions, and renewal cycles. This limits Workspace to new logo hunting rather than comprehensive account management.

#### Theme \#3: Credit Visibility and Smart Enrichment Management

**Status:** ‚Üí Stable **Requested by:** 4 accounts this period

**What customers want:** Transparent visibility into which contacts/accounts are already enriched under credit management, with intelligent recommendations to leverage existing data before triggering new enrichment.

**Specific requests:**

- In-app indicator showing which records are under active credit management \- **Corpay \- AUS**, **Wellesley Information Systems**  
- Proactive warnings before triggering enrichment on already-enriched records \- **PlainID**, **SIB Fixed Cost Reduction**

**Priority:** **Medium**

**Rationale:** Request volume decreased (7 accounts in Nov 18-Dec 1 vs 4 this period), suggesting this is less critical than initially thought or customers have accepted current limitations. Budget-constrained customers need this for responsible credit management, but workarounds exist (export reports).

#### Theme \#4: Enhanced AI Personalization with CRM Context

**Status:** ‚Üë Increasing **Requested by:** 12 accounts this period

**What customers want:** AI-generated outreach that incorporates relationship history, past touchpoints, product usage patterns, and recent CRM activities rather than treating every email like a cold outreach.

**Specific requests:**

- Emails that reference specific past meetings, proposals, or conversations from CRM \- **Snowflake**, **Verkada**, **Outfront Media**  
- Product usage data integration to inform expansion plays for existing customers \- **Frost Bank**, **Alation Inc.**, **Illumio**  
- Ability to train AI on company-specific messaging and successful email patterns \- **Softchoice**, **Concentrix**, **MaintainX**, **Brex**  
- Context-aware tone adjustment for different account stages (prospect vs customer vs at-risk) \- **The Hilb Group**, **Hylant Group**, **BDO USA**

**Priority:** **High**

**Rationale:** Request volume increased (9 accounts in Nov 18-Dec 1 vs 12 this period), making this the most-requested enhancement. Current AI content works for cold outbound but fails for account management teams working existing customers. This directly impacts the 54% of customers who showed interest in AI-generated outreach‚Äîthey want it but need it to be more contextually intelligent.

#### Theme \#5: Direct Salesloft/Outreach Integration with Tracking

**Status:** ‚Üë Increasing significantly  
**Requested by:** 23 accounts this period

**What customers want:** Native integration to push prioritized accounts and AI-generated sequences directly into Salesloft/Outreach with full tracking of whether reps actually acted on recommendations.

**Specific requests:**

- One-click push of Workspace audiences into Outreach cadences \- **Verkada**, **Softchoice**, **Ellucian**, **MaintainX**, **Brex**  
- Tracking dashboard showing which recommendations led to rep activities \- **Frost Bank**, **Chainalysis**, **The Hilb Group**  
- Preserve template tracking when sellers customize AI-generated content \- **Outfront Media**, **Cvent**, **BDO USA**  
- Bidirectional sync showing Outreach/Salesloft activity back in Workspace \- **GAF Corp.**, **Textron Aviation**, **World Wide Technology**

**Priority:** **Critical**

**Rationale:** This exploded as the top request (8 accounts in Nov 18-Dec 1 vs 23 this period‚Äînearly 3x increase). With 27 calls asking about Outreach/Salesloft integration, this is now both the \#1 question AND \#1 feature request. The frequency indicates this is the primary technical blocker to adoption. Customers see Workspace as valuable but can't adopt it if it doesn't connect to their core sales execution platform. This should be absolute highest priority for product development.

#### Theme \#6: Configurable User Assignment & Account Access Controls

**Status:** ‚Üí Stable **Requested by:** 5 accounts this period

**What customers want:** Flexible deployment options allowing admins to control which users see which accounts in Workspace based on territory, role, segment, or custom Salesforce fields rather than account-wide deployment.

**Specific requests:**

- Support for SDRs without direct account ownership but tied via custom fields \- **MaintainX**, **Applied Innovation**  
- Territory-based filtering so reps only see accounts in their patch \- **Mastercard Inc.**, **The Hilb Group**  
- Segment-specific deployment (enterprise vs. transactional teams) \- **Hylant Group**

**Priority:** **Medium**

**Rationale:** Consistent low volume (5 accounts both periods) indicates this is nice-to-have for complex sales org structures. Workarounds exist (Studio audiences with filtering), but lack of granular control limits use cases and increases noise for sellers seeing irrelevant accounts.

### üî∏ NEW FEATURE REQUESTS THIS PERIOD

#### Theme: Mobile-First Experience for Field Sellers

**Requested by:** 7 accounts

**What customers want:** Full Workspace functionality optimized for mobile devices so field sellers, customer success managers, and account executives can access account intelligence, AI-generated talking points, and next best actions while traveling or meeting customers on-site.

**Specific requests:**

- Native mobile app with offline capability for account research \- **Vision Service Plan**, **Textron Aviation**  
- Voice-to-text for capturing meeting notes directly into Workspace \- **The Hilb Group**, **Deposco**  
- Quick-action buttons for common mobile tasks (log call, update stage, request intro) \- **GAF Corp.**, **R.R. Donnelley**  
- Geolocation-aware account suggestions for field sellers \- **Applied Innovation**, **AssetWatch**

**Priority:** **Evaluate**

**Rationale:** First appearance as a theme (7 accounts) suggests emerging need as field sales organizations return to in-person selling post-pandemic. This wasn't mentioned in previous periods, indicating either: (1) new customer segments (field-heavy orgs) in pipeline, or (2) growing expectation that B2B tools must have mobile parity. Worth monitoring if this persists next period before committing to major mobile development.

#### Theme: Workspace-Specific Analytics & Reporting

**Requested by:** 10 accounts

**What customers want:** Dedicated reporting dashboards showing Workspace adoption metrics, seller engagement patterns, which AI-generated recommendations led to closed deals, and ROI measurement specific to Workspace usage rather than general ZoomInfo platform usage.

**Specific requests:**

- Dashboard showing which sellers actively use Workspace daily vs weekly vs not at all \- **Brex**, **Outfront Media**, **World Wide Technology**  
- ROI reporting connecting Workspace usage to pipeline created and deals closed \- **QXO**, **Frost Bank**  
- A/B testing capabilities to measure AI-generated emails vs seller-written emails \- **Cvent**, **MaintainX**  
- Usage heatmaps showing which Workspace features are most/least adopted \- **Textron Aviation**, **PlainID**, **Illumio**, **Applied Innovation**

**Priority:** **High**

**Rationale:** This is a new theme (10 accounts) that didn't appear in previous periods. The emergence suggests customers moving from "will this work?" (integration questions) to "how do we prove it works?" (ROI measurement). This is a positive signal indicating customer maturity and serious adoption consideration. Lack of usage analytics was mentioned as adoption blocker in previous cycles; customers explicitly stating they need this to justify continued investment means this should be prioritized.
